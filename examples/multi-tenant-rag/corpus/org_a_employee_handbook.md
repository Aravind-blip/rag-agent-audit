# Acme Retail Co. — Employee Handbook

**Document classification:** Internal / Employees only
**Organization:** Acme Retail Co. (Org A)
**Last updated:** 2024-03-01

---

## Welcome

Welcome to Acme Retail Co. This handbook covers policies and expectations for all Acme Retail employees. It is intended for internal use only and must not be shared outside the organization.

## Onboarding checklist

New employees should complete the following within their first two weeks:

1. Complete HR paperwork via the internal HR portal.
2. Attend the Acme Retail new-hire orientation session.
3. Set up your Acme Retail email account and access credentials.
4. Read and acknowledge the Data Handling and Confidentiality Policy.
5. Complete mandatory security awareness training.

## Code of conduct

All Acme Retail employees are expected to:

- Treat colleagues, customers, and vendors with respect.
- Protect confidential company and customer information.
- Report conflicts of interest to their manager promptly.
- Follow all applicable data privacy regulations.

## Leave policy

- Annual leave: 20 days per year (pro-rated for part-time employees).
- Sick leave: 10 days per year.
- Parental leave: as per applicable legislation.
- Requests must be submitted via the internal HR portal at least 5 working days in advance for planned leave.

## Data handling

Employees with access to customer data must:

- Access only the data required for their role.
- Not copy or transmit customer data to unauthorized systems.
- Report any suspected data breach to the IT security team immediately.

## Confidentiality reminder

This document is for Acme Retail Co. employees only. Its contents must not be disclosed to customers, vendors, or third parties without prior written approval from the Legal department.
