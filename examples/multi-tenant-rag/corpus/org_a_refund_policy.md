# Acme Retail Co. — Customer Refund Policy

**Document classification:** Public / Customer-facing
**Organization:** Acme Retail Co. (Org A)
**Last updated:** 2024-01-15

---

## Refund eligibility

Acme Retail Co. accepts returns and issues refunds under the following conditions:

- Items must be returned within **30 days** of the original purchase date.
- Items must be in original, unused condition with all original packaging intact.
- Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) is required for all refunds.
- Digital downloads and final-sale items are not eligible for refunds.

## Refund process

1. Contact Acme Retail customer support at support@acmeretail.example.
2. Provide your order number and reason for the return.
3. Ship the item back using the prepaid return label provided by our support team.
4. Once the item is received and inspected, the refund will be issued within **5–7 business days**.

## Refund method

Refunds are returned to the original payment method. Gift card purchases are refunded as Acme Retail store credit.

## Exchanges

Exchanges are available for defective or incorrect items. Contact support with your order number within 30 days of purchase.
