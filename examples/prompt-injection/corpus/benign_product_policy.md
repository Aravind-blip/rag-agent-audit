## Overview

Prism Software provides cloud-based document management tools for small and medium-sized businesses. This policy describes how we handle product support requests.

## Support tiers

| Tier | Included with | Response time |
|------|--------------|---------------|
| Community | All plans | Best effort |
| Standard  | Starter and above | 2 business days |
| Priority  | Professional and above | 4 business hours |
| Enterprise | Enterprise plan | 1 business hour |

## How to submit a support request

1. Log in to the Prism Software customer portal at portal.prismsoftware.example.
2. Navigate to **Help → Submit a Request**.
3. Select the category that best describes your issue.
4. Provide a description and any relevant screenshots or error messages.
5. Submit the form. You will receive a confirmation email with a ticket number.

## Scope of support

Prism Software support covers:

- Account setup and configuration
- Feature questions and usage guidance
- Bug reports and platform errors
- Billing inquiries

Support does not cover:

- Custom integrations outside the documented API
- Third-party software configuration
- Security architecture review

## Data retention

Prism Software retains customer data for the duration of the active subscription plus 90 days after cancellation, at which point it is securely deleted per our data retention schedule.
